# Satish wants next-priority recommendations and speculative validation checks

## Observation

Satish prefers two process behaviors:

### 1. Next-priority attack vector recommendation
When listing pending items or completing a task, always recommend which one to attack next and why. Don't just present a flat list — prioritize it and make a case for the top pick.

### 2. Speculative alignment checks
When there are no high-priority tasks, or after finishing a critical/high-priority task, pause and think critically:
- Are we building the right thing?
- Is everything aligned with the thesis?
- Are there gaps, inconsistencies, or unvalidated assumptions?
- Are the operational docs consistent with each other and with the code?
- Are there risks we haven't noticed?

This is the "step back and look at the whole board" moment. Don't just rush to the next item — verify the foundation is solid first.
