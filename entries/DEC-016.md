# Published package as 'vigil-risk' instead of 'vigil' due to name conflict

## Context

The development team was preparing to publish their risk intelligence tool to PyPI under the name 'vigil'.

## Choice

Decided to publish the package as 'vigil-risk' on PyPI instead of 'vigil'.

## Reasoning

The desired package name 'vigil' was already taken on PyPI, forcing the team to choose an alternative name that still clearly identifies the tool's purpose.

## Alternatives Considered

No specific alternatives mentioned in the commit history, but the team chose 'vigil-risk' which maintains brand identity while clearly indicating the tool's focus on risk assessment.
