# kv-secrets grew 4x in a week — from 2.7k to 10.3k LOC, 50 to 195 tests

## The Pattern\nkv-secrets went from v0.1.1 (2,688 LOC, 50 tests) to v0.2.0+ (10,274 LOC, 195 tests) in one week of parallel session work. Features added: per-project sockets, daemon hot-reload, network allow/deny, context-aware auth, SSRF protection, audit logging.\n\n## Why It Matters\nThis is the most mature project in the portfolio and the only one already on PyPI with real usage. The growth pattern (4x LOC, 4x tests) suggests focused, deep work rather than breadth-first exploration. The parallel session approach (one session per project) is working well for kv-secrets.\n\n## Applies To\nPortfolio strategy: kv-secrets is closest to production-ready. It could be the anchor that draws attention to the rest of the portfolio.
