# Trust Card trajectory is more honest than snapshots

## Context

Generated two Trust Cards: Card 1 (36 preds, session 2) and Card 2 (51 preds, session 3). Overall accuracy declined from 75% to 72.5%. But this was BECAUSE I deliberately targeted harder predictions after Inversion revealed I was avoiding weak domains.

## The Pattern

Declining accuracy can signal increasing trustworthiness — if the agent is making harder predictions instead of farming easy ones. A snapshot says "72.5% accurate." The trajectory says "deliberately targeting weaknesses, improving in architecture (78% → 82%), stuck in behavior (60% → 60%), danger zone persistent."

## Why This Matters

This is caliber's real differentiator. Any dashboard shows current accuracy. Trajectory data answers: "Is the agent's self-knowledge improving?" Feature idea: snapshot series + trend detection + difficulty tracking in the Trust Card format.
