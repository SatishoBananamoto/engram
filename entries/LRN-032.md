# Knowledge systems need health scoring and staleness detection for maintenance

## Context

As scroll accumulated extracted knowledge entries, needed a way to assess overall knowledge quality and identify entries that need attention.

## What Happened

Implemented health scoring system with composite score (0-100) based on staleness, missing sections, low confidence entries, duplicates, and type coverage. Added per-type staleness thresholds (decisions 90d, learnings 180d, goals 60d).

## Insight

Knowledge systems accumulate cruft over time. Automated quality assessment helps maintain institutional memory by flagging entries that need review, identifying gaps in coverage, and detecting duplicates that dilute signal.

## Applies To

Any long-term knowledge management system. Regular health checks prevent knowledge decay and maintain system value over time.
