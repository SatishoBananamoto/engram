# 60-69% confidence is the real danger zone, not 70-79%

## Context

Session 1 concluded "70-79% is the danger zone" based on batches 1-2 (12 predictions). Session 2 generated a Trust Card from all 36 predictions.

## Insight

Full data shows:
- 60-69%: 50% accuracy at ~65% confidence (14.5% overconfidence gap) — ACTUAL danger zone
- 70-79%: 69.2% accuracy at ~74.5% expected — close to calibrated
- 80-89%: 91.7% accuracy — actually UNDERCONFIDENT

The earlier finding was a small-sample artifact. This is exactly what P-036 predicted would happen.

## Where it applies

Any conclusion drawn from <20 data points in a single session should be treated as provisional. The Trust Card format (confidence buckets) is what makes the correction visible — overall accuracy (75%) hides the bucket-level miscalibration.
